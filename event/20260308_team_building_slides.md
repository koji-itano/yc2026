## Slide 1: Session Purpose

### English

**Session Purpose**
Build Globally Competitive Founding Teams

Our goal is not just forming teams for today, but shaping founding teams capable of winning in global markets.
We aim to upgrade existing teams to stronger configurations and help individuals find complementary co-founders.

**Global-from-Day-1**
Based on Y Combinator's Request for Startups (RFS), this event assumes a global scope from the start, rather than a domestic-only approach.
YC prioritizes founding team quality and complementarity over the product idea itself.
The presence of co-founders and their unique skill combinations are critical evaluation factors.

**Stronger Composition**
International members who can complement English communication, North American market understanding, and local networks significantly raise discussion quality and speed.
We strongly recommend including members with international backgrounds to build a robust team structure.

*Bonus points will be awarded during the review for team-based participation.*

## Slide 2: Flow and Timing

### English

**Flow and Timing**
Visualization, Connection, and Confirmation

**10:45 - 11:00 — Visualization**
- **Finalize form input**: Visualize everyone's status and interests.
- **Sticker on Name Tag**: Add status sticker (Team/Individual x Open/Close)
- **Interest Number**: Add/write your interest theme number on the name tag

**11:00 - 11:40 — Connection**
- **Networking**: Introduce yourself and explore ideas with nearby people/teams.
- **Team Updates**: Look for opportunities to update or compose teams.
- *Focus: Active discussion*

**11:40 - 12:20 — Confirmation + Pre-Work**
- **Finalize Teams**: If not yet confirmed, keep seeking team updates for a limited time.
- **Start Initial Work**: Confirmed teams begin: reread RFS, discuss direction, brainstorm initial ideas
